Join Our Team as a Data Analyst - Consumer Finance Team

At Wyndham Destinations, every day is an adventure where drive meets opportunity. Wherever you want to take your career, let’s get there together.

If you're a dedicated and skilled Data Analyst, here's your chance to be a driving force behind strategic decision-making, operational efficiency enhancement, and process optimisation.

As a member of our Consumer Finance team, your role will encompass the creation of insightful reports, execution of data-driven strategies, active participation in cross-functional projects, and the championing of continuous improvement initiatives.

How You’ll Shine

As a Data Analyst, your contributions will make a tangible difference. You will:

  • Collaborate effectively with stakeholders, aligning data-driven campaigns with business objectives and KPIs.
  • Apply advanced analytical techniques to ensure operational excellence and compliance with legal requirements.
  • Leverage data analysis to identify optimal contact times, driving efficiency and results.
  • Guide resource allocation strategies, contributing to cost reduction initiatives.
  • Play a vital role in cross-functional projects, enhancing process efficiency and effectiveness.
  • Test and implement data-driven systems, processes, and controls in alignment with company policies.
  • Translate complex findings into actionable recommendations, driving strategic decisions.
  • Foster transparent communication across cross-functional teams, fostering a culture of collaboration.
  • Showcase innovation and integrity in all aspects of your role.
  • Elevate the team through leadership values, effective communication, and respect for peers and managers.

What You’ll Bring

  • Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Outlook, and Word, with a commitment to continuous skills enhancement.
  • Hands-on experience with databases and SQL, coupled with data analysis and visualisation tool familiarity.
  • Strong critical thinking, analytical prowess, and adept problem-solving abilities.
  • Meticulous attention to detail, upholding thoroughness and accuracy.
  • A passion for process improvement and optimisation.
  • A willingness to embrace growth and change, actively advocating process improvement principles.
  • Outstanding communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Previous exposure to a contact center or finance environment is advantageous.
  • Self-motivation, exceptional time management, multitasking, and organisational competencies.
  • Flexibility and a willingness to undertake diverse tasks in a fast-paced setting.

How You’ll Be Rewarded

Build your career with a value driven organisation that promotes continual growth and development for its people. Some of our many benefits on offer include:

  • combination of work from home and office
  • newly-refurbished modern office space
  • professional development funding
  • discounted hotel stays across Australia, Fiji, New Zealand
  • subsidised private health cover upon successful completion of probation

Where You’ll Begin Your Journey

Wyndham Destinations Asia Pacific develops, markets and sells vacation ownership interests in a network of 51 resorts across Australia, New Zealand, Fiji, Bali, Thailand, Hawaii, Japan and Europe and serves more than 60,000 vacation owners in Club Wyndham South Pacific. Wyndham Destinations Asia Pacific is a division of Wyndham Destinations (NYSE: WYND) the world’s largest vacation ownership and exchange company with more than 25,000 associates worldwide. The Pacific corporate offices are located on the Gold Coast in Australia. Across the Asia Pacific region, we have offices in Singapore, Shanghai, Indonesia, Thailand, Japan and Clark in the Philippines.
